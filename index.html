lifeTime – Support Page

Welcome to lifeTime

a simple and focused app that helps you see time from a new perspective.

Enter your birthday and instantly visualize how many days remain in your life.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Does lifeTime require an internet connection?

No. The app works completely offline.

2. Why does the app only show the remaining lifetime?

The philosophy behind lifeTime is minimalism — to remind you that life is finite, without unnecessary distractions.

3. Is my birthday data stored or shared?

No. All data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Privacy Policy

  • lifeTime does not collect or share any personal data.
  • Birthday and related information are stored only on your device.
