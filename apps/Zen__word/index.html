Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I play Zen Word!?

Swipe your finger across the letter wheel to connect letters and form words. When you create a valid word that belongs to the puzzle, it automatically fills into the crossword grid. Your goal is to find all the words and complete the grid. The letters can be connected in any directionâ€”just keep swiping to spell out the word!

2. What are the different chapters?

Zen Word! features 20 beautifully themed chapters, each with unique visual styles inspired by times of day and nature:

  • Dawn to Midnight: 10 chapters following the cycle of a day (50-100 levels each)
  • Spring to Winter: 4 seasonal chapters (100 levels each)
  • Ocean, Forest, Mountain, Desert: Nature landscapes (100-200 levels each)
  • Aurora & Cosmos: The final mystical chapters (200 levels each)

3. What are bonus words?

Bonus words are valid English words you can form with the given letters that aren't part of the puzzle solution. When you discover a bonus word, you earn 2 coins and it's added to your personal word collection! You can view all your collected bonus words in the Words dictionary.

4. How does the coin system work?

Coins are the currency in Zen Word!:

  • Level completion: Earn 10 coins per level
  • Bonus words: Earn 2 coins per bonus word discovered
  • Chapter completion: Earn 50 coins bonus
  • Daily rewards: Earn 10-50 coins (7-day cycle)
  • Hints: Use 50 coins to reveal a letter

5. How do hints work?

When you're stuck, tap the hint button to reveal one letter in the crossword grid. Each hint costs 50 coins. Use hints strategically when you need a little help finding the right word!

6. How do streaks and daily rewards work?

Play every day to build your streak! The longer your streak, the more motivated you'll feel to continue. Daily rewards follow a 7-day cycle with increasing coin rewards (10â†’15â†’20â†’25â†’30â†’40â†’50), and on Day 7, you get a bonus 50 coins extra!

7. What achievements can I unlock?

Zen Word! features 11 achievements to pursue:

  • First Step: Complete your first level
  • Dawn Complete: Complete Chapter 1
  • Halfway There: Complete 1,000 levels
  • Legend: Complete all 2,000 levels
  • Speed Demon: Complete a level in under 30 seconds
  • Self-Reliant: Complete 5 levels without hints
  • Independent Mind: Complete 20 levels without hints
  • Weekly Warrior: Maintain a 7-day streak
  • Monthly Master: Maintain a 30-day streak
  • Bonus Hunter: Find 50 bonus words
  • Word Master: Find 200 bonus words

8. How do chapters unlock?

Chapters unlock progressively as you play. Complete 80% of the current chapter to unlock the next one. This allows you to move forward even if you get stuck on a few difficult levels.

9. Can I customize the experience?

Yes! In Settings, you can toggle haptic feedback on or off to customize your playing experience.
