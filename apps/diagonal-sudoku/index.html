Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is Diagonal Sudoku?

Diagonal Sudoku follows all standard Sudoku rules with an additional constraint: the two main diagonals (from corner to corner) must also contain the numbers 1-9 without repetition. This extra rule creates more challenging and satisfying puzzles that require deeper logical thinking.

2. What are the difficulty levels?

Diagonal Sudoku offers four difficulty levels to match your skill:

  • Easy: 40 initial numbers ‚Äî Perfect for beginners
  • Medium: 32 initial numbers ‚Äî A balanced challenge
  • Hard: 26 initial numbers ‚Äî For experienced players
  • Expert: 22 initial numbers ‚Äî The ultimate test

3. How does the Daily Challenge work?

Every day at midnight, a new puzzle is generated for all players worldwide. Complete the daily challenge to build your streak! The app tracks your current streak and best streak. Premium users can also access the past 30 days of challenges to catch up on any missed puzzles.

4. What are Exclusive Challenges?

Premium subscribers get access to three special challenge types that refresh regularly:

  • Weekly Expert Challenge: Expert difficulty, refreshes every Monday, +500 bonus points
  • Speed Challenge: Medium difficulty, must complete in under 5 minutes, refreshes every 3 days, +300 bonus points
  • Perfect Run Challenge: Hard difficulty, complete 3 games without errors, refreshes weekly, +750 bonus points

5. How does the hint system work?

When you're stuck, tap the hint button to reveal the correct number for an empty cell. The hint system intelligently selects cells using logical solving techniques. Free users get limited hints per game (5 on Easy, 4 on Medium, 3 on Hard, 2 on Expert), while Premium users enjoy unlimited hints.

6. What achievements can I unlock?

Diagonal Sudoku features 10 achievements to pursue:

  • First Victory: Complete your first puzzle
  • Speedster: Complete a puzzle in under 5 minutes
  • Marathon Runner: Play 10 games in a row
  • Streak Master: Maintain a 7-day daily challenge streak
  • Daily Champion: Complete 30 daily challenges
  • Expert Solver: Complete an Expert difficulty puzzle
  • Century Club: Complete 100 puzzles
  • Perfectionist: Complete 10 games without any errors
  • Hintless Hero: Complete 5 games without using hints
  • Dedication: Play the game on 30 different days

7. How does the ranking system work?

Your total score determines your rank. Progress through these levels:

  • Novice: Starting rank
  • Apprentice: 1,000 points
  • Solver: 5,000 points
  • Adept: 15,000 points
  • Expert: 35,000 points
  • Master: 75,000 points
  • Grandmaster: 150,000 points

8. How is my score calculated?

Your score is based on multiple factors:

  • Base score: Easy 100, Medium 200, Hard 400, Expert 800
  • Time bonus: Faster completion = higher multiplier (up to 2x)
  • Hint penalty: -10% per hint used
  • Error penalty: -5% per error made
  • Perfect bonus: +50% for no errors and no hints

9. What features are in Settings?

Customize your experience with these options:

  • Show Errors: Highlight incorrect entries in real-time
  • Show Timer: Display or hide the game timer
  • Auto-remove Notes: Automatically clear pencil marks when placing numbers
  • Highlight Same Numbers: Highlight all instances of a selected number
  • Haptic Feedback: Enable or disable vibration feedback
  • Language: Choose from 11 supported languages
