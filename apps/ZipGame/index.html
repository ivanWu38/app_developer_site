Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I play zip game!?

Start at the number 1 and draw a path connecting numbers in order (1, 2, 3, ...) until you fill the entire grid. You can only move to adjacent cells (up, down, left, right). The goal is to create a continuous path that visits every cell exactly once.

2. What are the different difficulty levels?

zip game! offers three difficulty levels:

  • Easy: 5√ó5 grid (25 cells) - Perfect for beginners
  • Medium: 7√ó7 grid (49 cells) - A moderate challenge
  • Hard: 9√ó9 grid (81 cells) - For puzzle masters

3. What is Daily Puzzle mode?

Every day, a new puzzle is available for all players. Complete the daily puzzle to maintain your streak. The difficulty varies each day to keep things interesting. You can replay the daily puzzle, but only your first completion counts toward your streak.

4. What is Practice mode?

Practice mode offers unlimited randomly generated puzzles. Choose your preferred difficulty and practice as much as you want. This is perfect for improving your skills without any pressure.

5. How do streaks work?

Complete the daily puzzle each day to build your streak. Your streak shows how many consecutive days you've completed the daily challenge. Miss a day and your streak resets to zero. Check the countdown timer to see when the next puzzle becomes available.

6. Can I undo my moves?

Yes! Simply lift your finger and start again from where you want to continue. You can also use the Reset button to start the puzzle from the beginning.

7. Does zip game! work offline?

Yes! zip game! works completely offline. All puzzles are generated locally on your device. You can play anytime, anywhere without an internet connection.

8. Can I change the app's appearance?

Yes! Go to Settings and choose your preferred appearance mode: Light, Dark, or Auto. Auto mode follows your device's system setting.

9. How do I turn off sounds or haptics?

Go to Settings to toggle Sound Effects and Haptics on or off according to your preference.

10. How do I reset my statistics?

Go to Settings > Reset All Statistics. Be careful - this action is permanent and will delete all your progress, streaks, and achievements.
