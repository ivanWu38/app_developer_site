Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I play Associations!?

You're presented with 16 words shuffled on a grid. Your goal is to find the 4 groups of 4 words that share a hidden connection. Tap on 4 words you think belong together and submit your guess. If correct, the category is revealed and the words are removed. Keep finding groups until you solve all 4 categories or run out of mistakes!

2. What do the colors mean?

Each category has a difficulty level indicated by color:

Yellow - Easiest
Green - Moderate
Blue - Tricky
Purple - Hardest

Yellow categories are straightforward (like "Fruits" or "Animals"), while purple categories often involve wordplay, obscure connections, or require creative thinking.

3. What is Daily Mode?

Every day, a new puzzle is released that's the same for all players worldwide. This creates a shared experience where you can compare your performance with friends. Daily puzzles can only be played once per day, and your streak tracks how many consecutive days you've played.

4. What is Practice Mode?

Practice Mode offers unlimited puzzles with three difficulty settings:

  • Easy: 2 straightforward + 2 moderate categories
  • Medium: Mixed difficulty across all four tiers
  • Hard: Mostly tricky and very tricky categories

Use Practice Mode to sharpen your skills without affecting your daily streak!

5. What does "One Away" mean?

When you submit a guess and see "One Away!", it means 3 of your 4 selected words belong to the same category. One word is incorrect. This hint helps you narrow down your next guess!

6. How does the time penalty work?

Your final time includes a 10-second penalty for each mistake. This encourages accuracy over speed:

  • A quick solve with mistakes may be slower than a careful, mistake-free run

7. What statistics are tracked?

The Journey tab shows comprehensive statistics including:

  • Streaks: Current streak and longest streak
  • Games played: Total daily and practice games
  • Perfect games: Games completed with zero mistakes
  • Best times: Personal records for each game type and difficulty

8. Can I customize the app?

Yes! You can customize the appearance including:

  • Light, Dark, or System appearance mode
  • Font themes and color themes
  • Premium subscribers unlock additional customization options
