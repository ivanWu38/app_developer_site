Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I play Connections~?

You are presented with a grid containing pairs of colored dots. Your goal is to draw paths connecting each pair of same-colored dots. Swipe or drag from one dot to create a path to its matching dot. The puzzle is complete when all dots are connected and the entire grid is filled - no empty cells allowed!

2. What are the rules for drawing paths?

  • Paths can only move up, down, left, or right (no diagonals)
  • Paths cannot cross other paths
  • Each cell can only be used by one path
  • All cells must be filled to complete the puzzle
  • Drag back along a path to undo it

3. How many levels are there?

Connections~ features 1,000 levels organized into 7 chapters with progressive difficulty:

  • Chapter 1: Getting Started (Levels 1-50) - Tutorial
  • Chapter 2: Beginner (51-150) - Easy
  • Chapter 3: Intermediate (151-300) - Medium
  • Chapter 4: Advanced (301-500) - Hard
  • Chapter 5: Expert (501-700) - Expert
  • Chapter 6: Master (701-900) - Master
  • Chapter 7: Grandmaster (901-1000) - Master

4. How does the star rating work?

Earn 1-3 stars based on your performance:

  • 3 Stars: Complete without hints and under the target time
  • 2 Stars: Complete under 1.5x the target time
  • 1 Star: Complete the level (any time)

5. What is the Daily Challenge?

A new unique puzzle is available every day. Complete the daily challenge to earn bonus hints - 1 hint for completion, or 2 hints for a perfect 3-star finish! Daily challenges help build your streak and keep you coming back for more.

6. How do hints work?

Hints automatically solve one incomplete path for you. Free players receive 4 hints daily, which reset each day. You can earn additional hints by completing daily challenges or watching ads. Premium subscribers enjoy unlimited hints.

8. What achievements can I unlock?

  • First Steps: Complete your first level
  • Century: Complete 100 levels
  • Speed Demon: Solve puzzles quickly
  • Perfectionist: Earn 3 stars on levels
  • Dedicated: Build a daily streak
  • Persistent: Keep playing
  • Master: Complete all 1,000 levels
  • Star Collector: Collect stars
  • No Hints: Complete levels without hints
  • Daily Warrior: Complete daily challenges

9. Does Connections~ work offline?

Yes! Connections~ works completely offline. All 1,000 levels are stored locally on your device. You can play anytime, anywhere without an internet connection.

10. Can I customize the appearance?

Yes! Go to Settings to choose your preferred appearance mode: Light, Dark, or System (follows your device setting). Premium subscribers can also customize dot color themes.

11. How do I turn off sounds or haptics?

Go to Settings to toggle Sound Effects and Haptics on or off according to your preference.

12. Can I reset my progress?

Yes. In Settings, you can reset all progress. This will clear all completed levels, stars, statistics, and achievements. Use with caution as this cannot be undone.
