Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I play connections~?

Connect pairs of matching colored dots by drawing paths between them. Your paths must fill every cell on the grid without overlapping. Swipe from one dot to its matching partner, navigating around other paths. Every puzzle has exactly one solution that fills the entire grid.

3. How does the chapter system work?

The game has 7 chapters with levels that progressively increase in difficulty:

  • Getting Started: Tutorial levels to learn the mechanics
  • Beginner: Easy puzzles to build confidence
  • Intermediate: Medium difficulty with more colors
  • Advanced: Harder puzzles with larger grids
  • Expert: Challenging levels requiring strategy
  • Master: Very difficult puzzles
  • Grandmaster: The ultimate test of your skills

Complete levels to unlock new chapters and progress through 1000 total levels.

4. How does the daily challenge work?

Every day you get a fresh expert-level puzzle (9√ó9 grid with 8 colors). Complete the daily challenge to earn bonus hints and maintain your daily streak. You can replay completed daily challenges for fun, but hints are only awarded on the first completion.

5. How does the star rating work?

Each puzzle is rated 1 to 3 stars based on your performance:

  • 3 Stars: Complete without using hints and with fast time
  • 2 Stars: Complete with minimal hints or moderate time
  • 1 Star: Complete the puzzle (any time or hints)

Collect stars to track your mastery and unlock achievements!

6. How do hints work?

When you're stuck, use a hint to reveal part of the solution. Hints show you the correct path for one color. You start with free hints and can earn more by completing daily challenges. Premium subscribers get unlimited hints.

  • Dedicated: Play for 7 days in a row
