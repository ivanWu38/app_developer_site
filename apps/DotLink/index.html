Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I play Dot Link!!?

Connect matching colored dots by drawing paths between them. Your goal is to fill every cell on the grid without crossing paths. Simply drag your finger from one dot to its matching pair, creating a continuous line that covers empty cells along the way.

2. What are the different difficulty levels?

Dot Link!! features 7 chapters with progressive difficulty:

  • Getting Started: 50 tutorial levels on 5√ó5 grids
  • Beginner: 100 easy levels
  • Intermediate: 150 medium levels
  • Advanced: 200 hard levels
  • Expert: 200 expert levels
  • Master & Grandmaster: 300 master-level challenges on 10√ó10 grids

3. How does the star rating system work?

Each level awards 1-3 stars based on your performance:

  • 3 Stars: Complete without hints and within the target time
  • 2 Stars: Finish within 1.5x the target time
  • 1 Star: Complete the level regardless of time

4. What is the Daily Challenge?

Every day, a fresh puzzle awaits! Complete the daily challenge to earn bonus hints (1 hint for completion, 2 hints for a 3-star finish) and maintain your daily streak. You can replay it for fun, but rewards are only earned on first completion.

5. How do hints work?

Hints help you solve tricky puzzles by automatically completing one random path. Using a hint adds 15 seconds to your time, which may affect your star rating. Earn hints through daily challenges or get unlimited hints with Dot Link Premium.

6. How do streaks work?

Play every day to build your streak! Your current streak shows consecutive days played, and your longest streak is recorded for posterity. The Journey tab displays your streak progress with a motivating flame indicator.

7. What achievements can I unlock?

Dot Link!! features 10 achievements to pursue:

  • First Steps: Complete your first level
  • Century: Complete 100 levels
  • Speed Demon: Solve a puzzle in under 10 seconds
  • Perfectionist: Get 3 stars on 10 consecutive levels
  • Dedicated: Maintain a 7-day streak
  • Persistent: Maintain a 30-day streak
  • Master: Complete all 1,000 levels
  • Star Collector: Earn 2,000 stars
  • Independent: Complete 50 levels without using hints
  • Daily Warrior: Complete 30 daily challenges

8. Can I customize the app's appearance?

Yes! Choose between Light, Dark, or Auto appearance modes in Settings. You can also select from multiple color themes for the dots (some themes are premium-only). Sound effects and haptic feedback can be toggled on or off.

9. Can I undo my moves?

Yes! Simply drag backward along your path to remove segments, or lift your finger and start drawing from where you want to continue. Use the Reset button to clear the entire puzzle and start fresh.

10. Does Dot Link!! work offline?

Yes! All puzzles are generated locally on your device using a smart procedural algorithm. You can play anytime, anywhere without an internet connection.
