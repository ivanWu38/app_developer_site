Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I play Connect Dots~?

Connect pairs of same-colored dots by drawing paths between them. The paths cannot cross each other, and you must fill every cell on the grid. Start by tapping a dot and drag to its matching pair. The goal is to connect all dot pairs while covering the entire board.

2. What are the difficulty levels?

  • Tutorial: 5√ó5 grid with 5 colors - Learn the basics

3. How does the star rating system work?

Each level awards 1 to 3 stars based on your performance:

  • 3 Stars: Complete within target time with no hints
  • 2 Stars: Complete within target time or use minimal hints
  • 1 Star: Complete the level

Replay levels anytime to improve your star rating!

4. What is the Daily Challenge?

Every day, a new unique puzzle becomes available for all players. Complete the daily challenge to maintain your streak and earn 3 bonus hints. The puzzle changes at midnight local time. You can replay it for fun, but rewards are only given on first completion.

5. How do hints work?

Hints help you when you're stuck by revealing the correct path for one dot pair. Free players receive 4 hints daily, while Premium subscribers enjoy unlimited hints. Complete the Daily Challenge to earn 3 additional hints!

6. How do streaks work?

Play the game each day to build your streak. Your current streak shows consecutive days played. The app also tracks your best streak ever. Missing a day resets your current streak to zero, but your best record is preserved.

7. What achievements can I unlock?

Connect Dots~ features 10 achievements:

  • First Steps: Complete your first level
  • Century: Complete 100 levels
  • Speed Demon: Complete a level in under 10 seconds
  • Perfectionist: Get 3 stars on 10 consecutive levels
  • Dedicated: Play for 7 days in a row
  • Persistent: Play for 30 days in a row
  • Master: Complete all 1000 levels
  • Star Collector: Collect 2000 stars
  • Independent: Complete 50 levels without hints
  • Daily Warrior: Complete 30 daily challenges

8. Can I customize the app's appearance?

Yes! Connect Dots~ offers 5 color themes (Classic, Neon, Pastel, Earth, Ocean) and 3 appearance modes (Light, Dark, System). Free users have access to the Classic theme, while Premium subscribers can unlock all themes.

9. Does Connect Dots~ work offline?

Yes! All 1000 levels are generated locally on your device. You can play anytime, anywhere without an internet connection. Only the Daily Challenge requires a brief sync to ensure all players get the same puzzle.

10. What languages are supported?

Connect Dots~ supports 15 languages: English, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese (Brazil), Italian, Russian, Arabic, Thai, Vietnamese, and Indonesian.
