Welcome to X_Sudoku

A modern, feature-rich Sudoku app with daily challenges, multiple difficulty levels, and comprehensive statistics tracking. Play offline, challenge yourself daily, and master the classic puzzle game.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Does X_Sudoku require an internet connection?

No. The app works completely offline. All puzzles are generated locally on your device.

2. What difficulty levels are available?

X_Sudoku offers four difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert. Each level provides a progressively challenging experience.

3. What are Daily Challenges?

Daily Challenges are special puzzles available once per day. Complete them to earn bonus points and build your streak. Compete with yourself to maintain the longest possible streak!

4. How do Statistics and Achievements work?

X_Sudoku tracks your gameplay statistics including games completed, win rate, average score, and best streak. Unlock achievements as you progress, and advance through player ranks from Novice to Grand Master.

5. What premium features are available?

Premium subscribers gain access to Advanced Statistics with detailed performance insights, Exclusive Challenges including Speed Run and Perfect Run modes, and an ad-free experience.

6. Can I use hints?

Yes. The hint system can help you when you're stuck. However, using hints affects your final score and certain achievements require completing puzzles without hints.

7. What is Pencil Mode?

Pencil Mode lets you add small candidate numbers to cells, helping you track possible solutions just like marking a paper Sudoku puzzle.

8. Which languages are supported?

X_Sudoku supports 11 languages: English, Japanese, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Spanish, German, French, Portuguese (Brazil), Korean, Italian, and Russian.

9. Does the app support Dark Mode?

Yes. The interface automatically adapts to your system's Light and Dark Mode settings.

10. Where is my game data stored?

All game data, statistics, and settings are stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server, ensuring your privacy.
