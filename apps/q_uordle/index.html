Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I play Quordle~?

Use the on-screen keyboard to type a 5-letter word, then press Enter to submit your guess. Your guess is applied to all four grids at once. Each grid has its own target word, so the same guess may show different color results on each grid. You have 9 attempts to solve all four words.

2. What do the colors mean?

After each guess, the letters change color to show how close your guess was:

  • Green: The letter is in the word and in the correct position.
  • Yellow: The letter is in the word but in the wrong position.
  • Gray: The letter is not in the word at all.

The keyboard also updates to show the best known state for each letter across all grids, helping you track which letters you've used.

3. What is Daily Mode?

Daily Mode presents the same four-word puzzle to everyone around the world each day. Complete it to maintain your streak and compare your results with friends! Your progress is automatically saved, so you can close the app and return to continue where you left off.

4. What is Practice Mode?

Practice Mode gives you unlimited random puzzles to sharpen your skills. Each game generates four new words, so you can play as many times as you want without waiting for a new daily puzzle.

5. How does the game track my statistics?

Quordle~ tracks your gaming statistics separately for Daily and Practice modes:

  • Games Played: Total number of games completed
  • Win Rate: Percentage of games where you solved all 4 words
  • Current Streak: Consecutive days you've won the Daily puzzle
  • Best Streak: Your longest winning streak ever

6. What happens when a grid is solved?

When you correctly guess a word, that grid shows a checkmark and becomes inactive. Your subsequent guesses will only apply to the remaining unsolved grids. The number displayed shows which guess number solved that word.

7. Can I share my results?

Yes! After completing a game, you can share your results showing which guess solved each grid (or X if unsolved). Share with friends to compare your performance!

8. Does the app support Dark Mode?

Yes! Quordle~ supports both Light and Dark modes. You can choose System (follows your device setting), Light, or Dark in the Settings tab.

9. Is Quordle~ available on iPad?

Yes! Quordle~ is optimized for both iPhone and iPad, with adaptive layouts that make the most of your screen size.

Tips for Success

  • Start with vowel-rich words: Words like AUDIO, ADIEU, or RAISE help identify vowels early.
  • Watch all four grids: A letter might be gray in one grid but green in another.
  • Use the keyboard colors: They show the best result across all grids.
  • Don't waste guesses: With only 9 attempts for 4 words, every guess counts!
  • Practice makes perfect: Use Practice Mode to develop your strategy.
