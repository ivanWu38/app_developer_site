Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How does Future Me work?

Simply write a letter to your future self, optionally attach up to 2 photos, set a delivery date, and schedule it. When the date arrives, you'll receive a notification and can open your letter in the Inbox.

2. Does Future Me require an internet connection?

No. The app works completely offline. All letters and photos are stored locally on your device.

3. Where are my letters and photos stored?

All letters and attached photos are stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

4. How is my privacy protected?

You can enable Face ID lock in Settings so only you can open the app and view your letters. Your content never leaves your device.

5. Can I change the delivery date of a letter?

Yes. You can reschedule a letter any time before it's delivered by tapping the "Reschedule" button in the letter detail view.

6. Will I get a reminder when my letter arrives?

Yes! Future Me sends you a notification when your scheduled delivery date arrives, so you never miss opening your letter.

7. Does it support Dark Mode?

Yes. The interface adapts to your device's Light and Dark Mode settings automatically.

8. Do I need an account?

No account or sign-in is required. Your letters are stored locally and are completely private.
