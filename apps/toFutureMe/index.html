toFutureMe — Support Page

Welcome to toFutureMe

A simple, privacy-first app to write a letter to your future self. Attach up to two photos, lock with Face ID, and open it when the day arrives.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Does toFutureMe require an internet connection?

No. The app works completely offline.

2. Where are my letters and photos stored?

All letters (and up to 2 attached photos per letter) are stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

3. How is my privacy protected?

You can enable Face ID lock so only you can open the app and view your letters. Your content never leaves your device.

4. Can I change the opening date of a letter?

Yes. You can adjust the future date any time before the letter is due.

5. Will I get a reminder?

Yes. toFutureMe will remind you when the selected date arrives so you can open your letter.

6. Does it support Dark Mode?

Yes. The interface adapts to Light and Dark Mode automatically.

7. Do I need an account?

No account or sign-in is required.

Privacy Policy

  • toFutureMe does not collect, share, or sell any personal data.
  • Letters and attached photos are stored only on your device.
  • Face ID lock is optional and can be enabled in Settings.
